who am I?

Hi, I am Aadya. I am a 20 yr old UI/UX designer and a graphic designer with a passion for all things design and creativity.
With a strong background in design, I always strive to improve and learn new skills and experiences

For as long as I can remember, I’ve always been into music, dance, and design. Dance was my first love and once I discovered it, everything changed. From practicing for hours, finding new songs, editing videos… I’ve tried everything.

Once I found Figma, UI/UX really clicked for me. I learnt how to build case studies and understood that good design isn’t just about looking nice but it’s about making things work. That’s why I picked up HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, so I could bring my designs to life through code.
